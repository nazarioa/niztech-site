me

There are few things that excite me more than building stuff and crafting beautiful lines of code. From a young age, I have always had a knack for breaking problems down so that I could understand them; I am intrigued by how things work. When I am in awe of someones work, my childhood passion makes me want to learn from others! Web development makes me smile and gives me this opportunity, so here I am!
